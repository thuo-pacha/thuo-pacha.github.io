Recipe#1:Chicken curry

  • Time: 30minutes_
  • Serves:3 _
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 2 table spoons
salt 1/2 tablespoon
onion 1 small one
minced cloves garlic 3
curry powder 2tablespoons
chicken broth 1 cup
coconut milk 14 oz
diced tomatoes 14.5 oz
tomato paste 2 table spoons
sugar 2 table spoons

Step 1: Cook the chicken:Heat the olive oil in an oven and the chicken to the dutch oven and season with salt and pepper.Cook for about 5 mins/until the chicken is no longer pink
step2:Finish the dish:Add the onion garlic curry powder,stir, cook for 2 more mins then add the chicken broth,coconut milk,tomatoes,,tomato paste and sugar.Stir everything,bring to a boil and cover with a lid .Turn heat down and simmer for another 15-20 minutes
step3:Garnish and serve:Garnish with parsely and serve over rice

Recipe#2:rice

  • time:30 minutes
  • serves:3 serves
ingredients Quantity
rice 1cup
water 2 cups
corn oil/olive oil 1 tablespoon
lemon juice 1 table spoon
salt 1 tablespoon

step 1:Boil the water and add salt,lemon juice and the corn oil/olive oil and stir.Add the washed rice and let it cook till the water finishes.Reduce heat and leave to cook for 15 minutes.Let it cool
Step 2:Serve with chicken curry

Recipe#3:Milk shake

  • time:5 minutes
  • serves:3
Ingredients Quantitity
ice cream 4 scoops
milk 1/4 cup
whipped cream
sprinkles or maraschino cherries

step1:add the ice cream and milk to a blender and blend until smooth.
Step 2:Pour into a large glass and top with whipped cream or your favourite toppings such as sprinkles or maraschino cherries.
SERVE AND EAT TO FILL
